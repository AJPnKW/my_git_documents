Venuiti Inventory and Table of Contents

One navigation page for the Venuiti company research, inferred org and people visuals, Technology Project Manager posting workspace, generated data, scripts, logs, and shareable packages stored in this repository.

Company structure and people relationships are inferred from public evidence and saved research data. They are not an official Venuiti org chart and do not assert legal ownership or reporting lines.
