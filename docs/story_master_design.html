Story Master Design

story_master.json is generated from the fact warehouse and stores reusable interview stories that are not company-specific.

  • Stable story IDs are derived from normalized role and theme slugs.
  • Each story retains source roles, employers, role-fit tags, audience-fit guidance, and sensitivity flags.
  • Preferred/avoid guidance is intended for downstream tailoring, not public surfacing.
