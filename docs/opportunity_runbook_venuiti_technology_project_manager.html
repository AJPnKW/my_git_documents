Opportunity Runbook

Execution guide for using the Venuiti Technology Project Manager opportunity layer in interview preparation and live interview capture.

Recommended Flow

  1. Read opportunity_profile.json first to internalize must-haves, hidden priorities, and sensitivity areas.
  2. Review opportunity_interview_response_bank.json and rehearse the primary stories in the listed order.
  3. Load live_interview_config.json before any live session to keep answers concise, specific, and consistent.
  4. Use the runbook questions below to pressure-test weak spots before the interview.

Primary Message Discipline

  • Lead with software delivery and technical coordination.
  • Use healthcare adjacency honestly, mainly through Sun Life benefits work.
  • Frame compliance and security background as a delivery-strengthener, not a role pivot.
  • Keep answers grounded in execution outcomes, not management abstractions.

Priority Stories

  • 1. Sun Life health and benefits delivery
  • 2. Manulife outsourced-project recovery
  • 3. Manulife internet-release delivery
  • 4. Spaenaur application portfolio leadership
  • 5. Hubble zero-downtime ERP upgrade

Likely Pressure Questions

  • Why this role and why Venuiti Healthcare?
  • How do you manage scope, budget, and timeline without slowing the team down?
  • How technical are you when working with developers?
  • What healthcare or regulated-environment experience translates here?
  • How do you handle a project that starts drifting or lacks structure?
  • Are you fully aligned with the Waterloo on-site model and security-clearance screening?

Use Rules During the Interview

  • Answer first, then give one anchored example.
  • Prefer one story per answer unless the interviewer explicitly asks for more.
  • Close by tying the example back to Venuiti's environment.
  • If uncertain, use a closest-parallel answer instead of guessing.

Validation Checklist

  • Confirm all three opportunity JSON files parse successfully before use.
  • Confirm the docs remain HTML-only artifacts.
  • Confirm the package records missing master-json inputs instead of silently inventing them.
  • Confirm the zip package includes only the required opportunity outputs and docs for this role bundle.
