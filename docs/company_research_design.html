Company Research Design

This subsystem provides deterministic, evidence-first company and people research outputs for interview support workflows. It is designed as a reusable normalization layer for future companies, not as a one-off Venuiti-specific scraper.

Folder Structure

  • data/schemas/company_people_research.schema.json stores the shared schema.
  • data/company_research/<group_slug>/ stores normalized outputs, logs, and package manifests.
  • scripts/company_research/ stores the reusable generation and packaging scripts plus curated source data.
  • docs/ stores HTML-only documentation.
  • .ai_downloads/ stores the shareable output zip.

JSON Purpose

  • company_people_research.json is the interview-facing normalized dataset.
  • source_index.json is the source ledger with URLs, domains, and extracted key points.
  • people_index.json is the normalized person list with affiliations and confidence.
  • entity_resolution.json stores alias handling, scored relationships, and rejects.

The Venuiti payload also includes two additive inferred-graph sections. org_structure_inferred models the public operating structure as an evidence-backed functional graph rather than a legal org chart. people_graph_inferred models person-to-entity and person-to-person associations using conservative relationship labels.

Validation Logic

The model uses explicit weighted signals instead of implicit merging.

  • Direct domain match.
  • Official domain cross-reference.
  • Explicit group language.
  • Shared location.
  • Shared employee overlap.
  • Shared founding year.

Weak signals are never allowed to convert into ownership claims. Shared people, geography, or founding year can support a candidate relationship, but they cannot independently prove legal ownership, control, or reporting lines.

For inferred graphs, confidence labels follow the same conservative posture. High confidence means the structure or association is explicitly stated on official sources. Medium confidence means multiple sources strongly imply the relationship. Low confidence means the relationship is preserved only as a lightweight inference. The model never emits reports_to edges without direct evidence.

Inferred Graphs

  • org_structure_inferred.nodes includes companies, brands, functions, departments, and teams with parent references, confidence, and evidence.
  • org_structure_inferred.relationships records inferred operating links such as belongs_to, operates_within, supports, and cross_function.
  • people_graph_inferred.nodes summarizes each known person's associated entities, likely functions, and overall mapping confidence.
  • people_graph_inferred.relationships uses only conservative labels such as associated_with, works_with, likely_same_team, and cross_entity.

These sections are meant for interview context and navigation, not for asserting legal ownership or managerial hierarchy.

Visual views are published as venuiti_org_chart.html and venuiti_people_graph.html. Both are static standalone HTML artifacts with zoom, pan, hover details, explicit inferred-status warnings, and layout scaling tuned for denser people and team graphs.

Required node fields remain stable. org_structure_inferred.nodes must emit id, name, type, parent_id, description, confidence, and evidence. people_graph_inferred.nodes must emit person_id, name, associated_entities, likely_functions, and confidence.

Relationship records are also fixed. org_structure_inferred.relationships must emit from_id, to_id, relationship_type, confidence, and evidence. people_graph_inferred.relationships must emit from_person_id plus either to_person_id or to_entity_id, along with relationship_type, confidence, and evidence.

The inference rules stay deliberately conservative: derive structure from official sites, brand separation, service lines, job-posting patterns, domain specialization, and repeated public people-title snapshots; derive people links from repeated co-occurrence, shared entity association, and shared function. The model must not invent reporting chains and should prefer associated_with, works_with, and likely_same_team when evidence is indirect.

Confidence Model

  • >= 0.80: accepted high confidence.
  • 0.60 - 0.79: accepted with caution.
  • 0.45 - 0.59: candidate only.
  • < 0.45: rejected weak match.

This keeps the subsystem safe for interview support, where documented uncertainty is preferable to overstated structure.

Reusable Design Choices

  • Group data is curated in Python structures, so future companies can be added without changing the output contract.
  • The schema is shared and group-agnostic.
  • The generator writes deterministic JSON with stable ordering and snapshot dates.
  • Build and package runs emit .log.txt files.
  • Packaging is deterministic and limited to the relevant outputs and docs.
