Career Master Design

career_master.json is the warehouse of normalized facts across 13 employers and 19 roles.

  • Structured workbook rows are treated as high-confidence chronology anchors.
  • Historical resumes and source documents enrich tools, frameworks, achievements, and duplicate evidence traces.
  • 3 role records are hidden by default because they are short-tenure, awkward, seasonal, or selectively relevant.
  • Duplicate source evidence is preserved inside each role and employer record instead of collapsed away.
