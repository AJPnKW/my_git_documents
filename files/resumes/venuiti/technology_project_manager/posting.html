About Venuiti Healthcare

Venuiti Healthcare specializes in elevating the healthcare experience through custom software development and comprehensive integration solutions. With nearly two decades of industry expertise, teams in Waterloo, Canada, and Atlanta, Georgia build pioneering technologies tailored to the needs of healthcare professionals and institutions.

This role supports an established healthcare application that plays a vital role in delivering digital health solutions. You will work closely with developers, business stakeholders, and healthcare partners to ensure the continued success and evolution of the platform.

If you're passionate about building software that has a meaningful purpose, we’d love to talk to you.

Role Overview

The Technology Project Manager (Technical Project Manager) oversees the full lifecycle of software development projects in the health‑tech space. You will be accountable for scope, budget, timelines, communication, documentation, and cross‑functional coordination across all phases of execution.

Key Responsibilities

  • Manage projects with top‑down oversight throughout the entire execution lifecycle.
  • Ensure success as defined by adherence to scope, budget, and timeline.
  • Develop comprehensive project plans that merge company goals with consumer requirements.
  • Coordinate managers, developers, technical personnel, and external partners across all phases.
  • Monitor project progress continuously and produce detailed scheduled reports.
  • Track milestones, deliverables, and measurable items.
  • Communicate proactively with team members and stakeholders.
  • Identify problems early, create solutions, and implement efficiency improvements.
  • Review proposed modifications to project plans and meet with stakeholders to approve changes.
  • Perform and maintain complete project documentation.
