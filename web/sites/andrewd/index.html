Support Journey

Use this order to move through the information.

StageQuestionWhy it mattersWhere to go
1. Current situationWhat support do you already have?Avoids repeating short-term supports that did not work.
2. ODSP workerIs ODSP helping with employment supports?ODSP may open faster referral paths.
3. DSO eligibilityIs DSO active, incomplete, or unknown?DSO may unlock developmental-service supports and Passport funding.
4. Job-retention supportDo you need support after hiring?Repeated short employment points to workplace support, training, coaching, and fit.
5. Education repairDid college/training fail because supports were missing?Accessible Learning should be involved before another attempt.
