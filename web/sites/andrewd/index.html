ODSP: What to Ask

Main point: ask for employment supports focused on keeping work, job coaching, and service-provider referral.
  • Ask who the assigned caseworker is.
  • Ask for ODSP Employment Supports or referral to the correct Employment Ontario/disability employment provider.
  • Ask what supports can help with job retention, job coaching, accommodations, transportation, training, and work-related disability costs.
  • Ask how working affects ODSP income reporting, benefits, and health coverage.
  • Ask for written next steps by email or letter.

Passport Funding

Passport is reimbursement funding for eligible adults with a developmental disability. It may help pay for community participation, daily living supports, person-directed planning, caregiver respite, and some fee-for-service supports through agencies.

  • Ask DSO if Passport eligibility or funding exists.
  • Ask KW Habilitation if Passport can be used for KW Career Compass supports.
  • Ask whether person-directed planning can help build a practical work/life roadmap.

Conestoga / Education Return Path

Best order: Accessible Learning first, then academic guidance, then program/course decisions.
  • Complete the Accessible Learning intake form before returning.
  • Ask what documentation is needed for ADHD, learning disability, developmental disability, or mental-health barriers.
  • Ask whether a reduced course load, re-entry plan, upgrading, or alternate pathway makes sense.
  • Ask for accommodations before classes start.

Accommodation Rights

Use carefully: the first goal is better support and prevention of another failed placement, not escalation.
  • In Ontario, employers and education providers have a duty to accommodate disability-related needs to the point of undue hardship.
  • The person should communicate restrictions or needs as best as possible, preferably in writing.
  • Generally, accommodation requests should focus on functional needs, not detailed diagnosis information.
  • For legal support, contact the Human Rights Legal Support Centre.
