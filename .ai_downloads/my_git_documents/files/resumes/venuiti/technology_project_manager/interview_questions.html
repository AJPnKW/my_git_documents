11. How do you handle change requests or shifting priorities?

Possible

I try to make the trade-offs visible early. A change is not just a request; it affects scope, timing, dependencies, testing, and sometimes cost. My role is to make that impact clear, help the right people decide, and then re-baseline the work in a controlled way.

12. How do you approach project documentation?

Possible

I see documentation as part of delivery, not an afterthought. Good documentation keeps teams aligned, makes handoffs cleaner, supports stakeholder visibility, and reduces confusion later. A lot of my work has involved status reporting, project artifacts, change records, dashboards, and documentation in regulated environments.
